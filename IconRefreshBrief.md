# Icon Refresh Brief

Recommend a light evolution of the current app icon under a "Calibrated Convert" concept rather than a full replacement.

- Use a blue liquid-glass background with fewer, cleaner background blobs so the symbol reads faster.
- Make the opposing conversion arrows larger and more central to improve recognition at small sizes.
- Add subtle measurement tick marks around or behind the arrows to suggest precision without clutter.
- Increase contrast between the arrows, ticks, and background so the icon holds up in small iPhone and iPad contexts.
- Keep the overall silhouette and visual intent familiar so the refresh feels like the same app, just sharper and more legible.
